Help Center

Setting up a Sale

Creating the Sale

  1. Head to the Sales page and click "CREATE SALE"
  2. Fill out the details about your sale, then click "CREATE SALE"
  3. An empty Sale will now be created. It is not yet open

Adding Items to the Sale

  1. Click "ADD ITEM" on the sale page
  2. Fill out the details about your item, then click "ADD ITEM"

Opening the Sale

  1. Click "EDIT SALE" on the sale page
  2. Click "OPEN SALE"

Managing a Sale

User Roles

  • Admin - Can do anything on the sale
  • Bookkeeper - Can view financial records
  • Cashier - Can create transactions
  • Clerk - Can view and print tags
  • Seller - Can update the catalog, view financial records, create transactions, and view and print tags

Managing Users

  1. Click "EDIT SALE" on the sale page
  2. Under "User Roles" is a list of current users and their roles
  3. To add a user, type a username and select their role from the menu, then click "ADD USER"
  4. To remove a user, Click "Remove" in the User Roles table

Closing the Sale

If you wish to stop accepting transactions and hide the sale, you can close it.
  1. Click "EDIT SALE" on the sale page
  2. Click "CLOSE SALE"

Archiving the Sale

When the sale is finished and you wish to stop allowing new transactions, or any changes, you can archive the sale.
  1. Click "EDIT SALE" on the sale page
  2. Click "ARCHIVE SALE"

QR Code Checkout

As a cashier, you can use your mobile device to speed up the checkout process.

To get started, download a QR reader app. For iOS, the Scanbot app is recommended. Other apps may redirect to a web browser and slow your checkout workflow.

Steps:

  1. Open a Sale page.
  2. Click "CREATE TRANSACTION".
  3. Using your mobile device, scan the QR code on the Transaction page. This registers your device.
  4. Now, scan the QR code of each Item you wish to add.
